**Liquid asset:**
Resources that can be quickly and securely converted into cash without a significant loss in value.
Examples:
- **Cash & Bank Deposits:** Physical currency, checking accounts, and savings accounts.
- **Marketable Securities:** Stocks, bonds, ETFs, and mutual funds that trade on public exchanges with high daily volume.
